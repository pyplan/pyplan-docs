How to Sign up¶

Signing up allows you to access Cubeplan. To log in, click the following URL: Cubeplan

Cubeplan page will open, it has the following format:

Enter your user name and password and click

If you enable the option , the system will remember your user name for your future use.

Select company¶

In case your user is associated with more than one company, you must select the company in which you are going to work right after you sign in.

Once you selected one, click .

Reset password¶

If you forgot your password, you can reset it by clicking .

An e-mail will be send to your e-mail address with the following link: When you click it, a new e-mail with be sent to you containing the new password.

Edit profile¶

Click Edit profile and the following window will open:

To change your profile picture, click .
You can also edit your user name, e-mail address, and password.

When you finish, click to introduce the changes.
